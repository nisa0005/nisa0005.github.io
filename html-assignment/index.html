About Me

Hello! My name is Arzin Nisar. I was born on December 17th, 2005, in Ottawa, Canada. I was born into a family consisting of my older brother, my father, and my mother. Shortly after my birth, my family and I moved to Dubai. I lived there for about 5 years in the scorching heat, until we moved to Toronto and then finally returned to Ottawa. I don't remember much about my childhood, but something that has remained consistent throughout my years is my love for cats!

Today, I am 17 years old. I graduated from WCSS and I am now attending Algonquin College. In my free time, I enjoy participating in visual art activities. I typically stick to drawing humans, but I do enjoy drawing animals from time to time. Some other hobbies of mine are building Legos, gaming, reading, and petting my cat.

Ultimately, every human is their own writer. - Han Sooyoung
